BILL SOLLY (1931-2020) is the author/composer/lyricist of the musicals Boy Meets Boy, The Great American Backstage Musical, and many others, including the popular children's musical, The Cat in the Castle.

On this website you will find information regarding the songs, excerpts from reviews, and the names of the licensing agents for all of his musicals and songs.
