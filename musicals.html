THE GREAT AMERICAN BACKSTAGE MUSICAL
Book by Bill Solly and Donald Ward
Music and lyrics by Bill Solly

It premiered in Los Angeles in 1976, and has since been performed in theatres all over the USA, and won rave reviews in London at the Regency Theatre in 1978. Performances for The Great American Backstage Musical, in the USA and elsewhere (except New York City) are licensed by Samuel French, 45 West 25th Street, New York, NY 10010 - also Hollywood, London, and Toronto.
